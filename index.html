Jason Thornton

Freelance Technical Writer

I'm a freelance technical writer who specializes in developer documentation. I graduated from West Virginia State University in May 2019 with a Bachelor of Arts in professional writing. After graduating, I took an interest in web development and learned JavaScript, HTML, and CSS independently. From there, I learned the fundamentals of modern web applications, including what APIs are and how to build them. As a result of this, I'm able to take code and translate it into terms that users can understand.

Whether it's in writing or code, I'm more concerned with what something is and if it's communicated clearly than I am with what happens "under the hood." For me, technical writing is the natural intersection of those two things. It's a philosophy that I bring to every one of my projects.
